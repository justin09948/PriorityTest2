About Us

We understand that your car is not just a mode of transportation, it is an investment and reflection of your life style That's why here at Priority, we strive to offer more than just a basic cleaning service. Our team of dedicated students is committed to providing attention to detail and ensuring that every inch of your vehicle shines like new. By choosing Priority, you're not just getting a clean car, you're supporting a business run by passionate college students who are dedicated to excellence, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

Services

1

Car Wash/Detail Packages

At Priority, we offer three packages: Primary (exterior pressure wash, exterior hand wash, tire cleaning, window cleaning), Priority (Primary plus interior vacuuming, dashboard cleaning, wheel detailing, wax, door cleaning), and Prestige (Priority plus deep interior cleaning, leather treatment, polishing, interior shampooing). Choose the best fit for your car's needs.

2

Priority Package

Our three maintenance packages consists of the Primary, Priority, and Perfect packages. Our Basic Wash Package includes an monthly Primary Wash plus an interior wipe down. The Priority Wash Package adds a monthly Priority Wash plus a wax and tire application every 3 months. Our Perfect Wash Package includes a full Perfect Detail plus seasonal protectants. Choose the package that best suits your car's needs for a spotless and well-maintained look.

3

Full Detail

Soon we will be adding an Add-On Package. Upgrade your car wash by selecting from a range of premium services tailored to your needs. Whether it's a pet-hair removal, headlight restoration, or an odor elimination treatment, our Add-On Package offers the flexibility to customize your wash and leave your vehicle looking and feeling its best.
